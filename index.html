Akan Names

Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture.In Ghana, children are given their first name as a 'day name' which corresponds to the day in the week they were born.

This is web application that takes a your birthday and calculates the day of the week that you were born and then depending on their gender outputs your Akan Name.
